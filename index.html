I am a mathematician working in mathematical logic, more specifically in set theory and set-theoretic model theory. I am currently a research associate ("wissenschaftlicher Mitarbeiter") in the Logic Group of the Department of Mathematics at the University of Hamburg. I recently defended my PhD thesis Interactions between Large Cardinals and Strong Logics. You can access the thesis here.
